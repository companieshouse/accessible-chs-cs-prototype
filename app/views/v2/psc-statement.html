What is a PSC statement?
If a company has no people with significant control (PSCs), or is currently investigating who its PSCs are, it needs to file a statement to explain why its full PSC information is not yet on the public register. For example, if there is a PSC that has not yet been identified, or if a PSC has been identified but there details have not yet been confirmed.
Read the guidance for a full list of PSC statements.
